iCareManager Voice Discovery Session — Informed Consent and Recording Authorization

Effective Date: April 2026

Please read this carefully before starting your session.

Section 1 — Nature of This Session

This voice session is conducted by an artificial intelligence assistant developed and operated by iCareManager. You are not speaking with a human employee. The AI assistant is designed to ask questions about your organization's current workflows, processes, staffing, and technology use for the purpose of supporting your organization's implementation of the iCareManager platform.

Section 2 — Recording and Transcription

By proceeding, you expressly consent to the recording, transcription, and storage of this session. Your voice and responses will be captured, converted to text, and retained by iCareManager for the purposes described in this agreement.

Section 3 — How Your Information Will Be Used

The information you provide during this session will be used exclusively for the following purposes: to understand your organization's current workflows and operational processes; to configure and customize the iCareManager platform for your organization's specific needs; to prepare iCareManager's implementation team for analysis and planning calls with your organization; and to generate workflow documentation and implementation planning materials. Your responses will not be sold, shared with third parties, used for marketing purposes, or used to train AI models that are made available to other customers. iCareManager may use anonymized and aggregated insights to improve the quality of its discovery process, but no identifying information about your organization will be used in any externally facing capacity.

Section 4 — Your Authority to Participate

By proceeding, you represent and warrant that you are an authorized representative of your organization and that you have the authority to share information about your organization's internal workflows, processes, systems, and operations with iCareManager for the purposes of this implementation engagement.

Section 5 — No Collection of Client or Patient Data

Do not share the names, diagnoses, social security numbers, or any other personally identifiable information about the individuals your organization serves. This session is intended to capture organizational and operational information only. You are solely responsible for ensuring that no protected health information or other regulated personal data is disclosed during this session.

Section 6 — Data Security

iCareManager maintains administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information you share. Session transcripts are stored in a secured environment accessible only to your assigned iCareManager implementation team.

Section 7 — Right to Stop

You may end this session at any time by closing your browser. Partial sessions may be saved to allow you to resume at a later time. You may request deletion of your session data by contacting help@icaremanager.com.

Section 8 — Contact

If you have questions about how your information is used or wish to request deletion of your session data, contact: help@icaremanager.com

By checking the acknowledgment box and clicking I Agree — Start My Session, you confirm that you have read and understood this consent, that you agree to its terms, and that you consent to the recording and use of your responses as described above.
